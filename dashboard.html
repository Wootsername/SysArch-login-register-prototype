Rules and Regulation
  • Maintain silence, proper decorum, and discipline inside the laboratory.

  • Follow all instructions from the instructor or lab administrator.
  • Use computer-related games and other distracting activities are prohibited.
  • Always log your session properly before leaving the laboratory.
  • Keep your profile information updated so the student record stays accurate.
